REGISTRATION

Registration for the hackathon is free and open for MRI researchers at all career levels (ranging from students to senior faculty). We require that you are affiliated with an institution or cooperation involved in MRI research and that you have prior experience in MRI.

Please submit your registration by completing the application form that will appear after the Recaptcha challenge. Please note that if there should be more applications than open spots in the projects teams, we will select participants based on the aim of achieving a wide diversity among the participants (unfortunately, there is a limit on the maximum number of participants that we can host).

The registration deadline is September 1st, 2023. We will be in touch shortly afterwards and inform you if your registration has been accepted.

Our hackathon will engage the MR engineering community toward development of an open-source low-field MRI system. In only four days, we will create a full-fledged MRI scanner, enabling new imaging approaches and dissemination of the know-how needed for scanner development.
